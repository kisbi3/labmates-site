Asterelle Changelog

What actually changed.

A user-facing record of new capabilities, improvements, and fixes—not a dump of internal commits.

Asterelle is currently in private beta. These entries describe changes in development builds, not commitments to a public release version.

July 15, 2026

Asterelle identity and product story

Private beta · In development

New

  • Applied the new Asterism mark and horizontal Asterelle wordmark across the site and desktop application.
  • Added Korean and English landing pages, public Docs, and Changelog routes.
  • Added a multi-model explanation for using configured providers and local models inside one project context.

Improved

  • Reframed the landing page around resuming research context, reviewing evidence, and human ratification.
  • Clarified supported operating systems and existing model access required for early access.
  • Separated exploratory Roadmap items from current product capabilities.

Fixed

  • Removed remaining LabMates branding from the empty editor, graph, settings, and Mates surfaces.
  • Allowed the Source Control label to wrap onto two lines in the expanded activity rail.

July 14, 2026

Observatory for personal research

Desktop private beta

New

  • Added Observatory to collect changes and pending decisions from multiple projects in one view.
  • Introduced Lab signals, Continue working, Projects at a glance, and Needs your attention modules.
  • Connected project AI signals and Git evidence to the selected decision review flow.

Improved

  • Project chips now narrow project rows and decisions without replacing the whole view.
  • Recent activity stays collapsed and starts syncing only when opened.
  • Reorganized the previous Morning Briefing functions into purpose-specific personal research modules.
